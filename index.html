Morteza Taiebat

PhD Candidate

University of Michigan

I am a PhD Candidate at the Univeristy of Michigan, School for Environment & Sustainbility and Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering. I am affiliated with the Center for Sustainable Systems, working under supervision of Ming Xu, Sam Stolper, and Henry Liu.

Research

My interest lies in the system-level analysis of the emerging modes of transportation (i.e. automated, shared, & electric vehicles) and understanding how they impact travel pattern, energy use, and economics of mobility. My research broadly focuses on leveraging these mobility modes to improve transportation system efficiency while reducing social inequality and environmental impacts. I utilize data science techniques, economics, and optimization to investigate the mechanisms that promote sustainable transportation.
